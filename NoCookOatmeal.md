No cook oatmeal
===============

Ingredients
-----------

*   1 1/3 c milk
*   1 c rolled oats
*   1 c Greek yogurt
*   3T chia seeds
*   3T honey
*   1T ground cinnamon

Instructions
------------

Combine milk, oats, Greek yogurt, chia seeds, honey, and cinnamon in a
1/2-pint jar with a lid; cover and shake until combined. Cover jar with lid.
Refrigerate oatmeal, 8 hours to overnight.
