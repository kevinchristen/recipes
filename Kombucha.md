# Kombucha
From [this Laesk YouTube video](https://youtu.be/LmsVReoRIDI?si=3kfUZNzsbFSd3GQe).

## Ingredients (for about 3.5L)

* 32 gm tea (8 gm tea/liter of brewed kombucha)
* 280 gm sugar
* 500 mL (16 oz) finished kombucha

## Instructions

1. Boil 1L water and let it sit for 2 min to partially cool.
2. Steep tea for 12 min, stirring.
3. Strain tea.
4. Mix sugar into tea until it dissolves.
5. Poor sweetened tea into fermentation jar.
6. Add 2.4L water and kombucha.
7. Cover with breathable cloth and store in warm area.
