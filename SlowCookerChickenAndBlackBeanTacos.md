# Slow Cooker Chicken and Black Bean Tacos

From [Skinny Taste](https://www.skinnytaste.com/slow-cooker-chicken-black-bean-tacos/). Recommended by Cheryl Nieuwesteeg. Total time: 5 hrs.

## Ingredients

* Kosher salt
* 16 oz raw skinless chicken breast
* 2 15 oz cans black beans
* 2 10 oz cans Rotel Diced Tomatoes and Green Chiles
* 1 T chili powder
* 2 t ground cumin
* 1/2 t garlic powder
* 1/2 t oregano
* 1 medium scallion, diced
* 1 T chopped cilantro, optional

## Instructions

1. Season chicken with salt
2. Add the drained beans, tomatoes, and spices to the crock pot
3. Place chicken in the crock pot and cover
4. Cook on low for 4-5 hours
5. Remove chicken from the crock pot and shred
6. Add shredded chicken to the crock pot to keep warm
